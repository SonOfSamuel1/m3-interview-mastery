## Page 2: Instructions

### How to Use This Assessment

This assessment evaluates your fit across three dimensions:

1. **Sales Environment Preferences** - What type of sales role energizes you?
2. **Skill Confidence** - How confident are you in core AE competencies?
3. **Values Alignment** - What do you need from your career to be fulfilled?

### Scoring
- Answer honestly based on who you are TODAY, not who you want to become
- There are no "right" answers - only answers that reveal your fit
- Score each section and total them at the end
- Use the interpretation guide to understand your results

### What Your Results Mean

| Score Range | Interpretation |
|-------------|----------------|
| 85-100 | Strong Fit - You're well-aligned with high-paying AE roles |
| 70-84 | Good Fit - Minor gaps to address, but fundamentally aligned |
| 55-69 | Moderate Fit - Some areas need development or reconsideration |
| Below 55 | Potential Mismatch - Consider if this is truly the right path |
