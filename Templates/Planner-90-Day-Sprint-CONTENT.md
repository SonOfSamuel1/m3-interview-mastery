### Content

**Welcome to Your 90-Day Sprint**

This planner breaks your job search into 4 phases across 13 weeks. Each phase has specific goals and activities. Use this planner to:

1. Track your weekly activities and progress
2. Monitor your conversion rates to identify bottlenecks
3. Stay accountable to your goals
4. Course-correct when something isn't working

---

| Phase | Weeks | Focus | Goal |
|-------|-------|-------|------|
| Foundation | 1-2 | Profile, positioning, materials | Job search assets ready |

| Interview Momentum | 7-10 | Interviews, follow-ups, negotiation prep | 5+ interviews scheduled |

**Weekly Rhythm**

Each week, you should:
- Set 3-5 specific goals at the start
- Log your activities daily
- Review metrics at end of week
- Identify 1 thing to improve next week

In the Weekly Tracker tab:

- Mark status for each activity
- Add notes on outcomes

## Page 2: Understanding the 90-Day Sprint

### The 4 Phases

**Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-2)**
Before you start applying, you need your positioning and materials dialed in. Rushing past this phase leads to wasted effort later.

Key activities:
- Complete self-assessments
- Define target role criteria
- Create/update resume and LinkedIn
- Build target company list
- Set up tracking system

Success metric: All materials ready, 20 target companies identified

---

**Phase 2: Pipeline Building (Weeks 3-6)**
This is where you fill your funnel. The goal is to create enough opportunities that you have options. Most people don't do enough volume here.

Key activities:
- 20+ targeted outreach messages per week
- 10+ applications per week
- Network activation
- Initial conversations

Success metric: 20+ active conversations, first interviews scheduled

---

**Phase 3: Interview Momentum (Weeks 7-10)**
Now you're converting pipeline to interviews. Your focus shifts from quantity to quality - preparing deeply for each conversation.

Key activities:
- 3-5 interviews per week
- Deep company research
- Mock interview practice
- Relationship building with hiring teams

Success metric: 5+ interview processes active, 2+ at final stages

---

**Phase 4: Closing (Weeks 11-13)**
The final push. You're managing multiple offers, negotiating, and making your decision. Don't rush - this is where the money is made.

Key activities:
- Final round interviews
- Offer evaluation
- Negotiation
- Decision and transition

Success metric: Signed offer at target compensation

## Page 3: How to Use the Spreadsheet

### Weekly Workflow

**Monday: Plan**
- Review Master Plan for the week's focus
- Set 3-5 specific goals in Weekly Tracker
- Block time for key activities

**Daily: Execute & Log**
- Complete activities according to plan
- Log each activity in Weekly Tracker
- Note next steps for every conversation

**Friday: Review**
- Check Metrics Dashboard
- Complete weekly reflection
- Adjust approach for next week if needed
