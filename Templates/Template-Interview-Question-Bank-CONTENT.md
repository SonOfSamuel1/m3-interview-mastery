## Tab 1: Instructions

### Header
**How to Use the Interview Question Bank**

### Content

**This question bank helps you:**
1. Prepare answers for common questions BEFORE interviews
2. Know what interviewers are really evaluating
3. Ask smart questions that impress hiring managers
4. Build a library of your own stories

---

**Before Each Interview:**
1. Filter questions by Category and Interview Stage
2. Review the "What They're Really Asking" column
3. Prepare your answer using suggested Framework
4. Practice out loud (not just in your head)

**After Each Interview:**
1. Note which questions came up
2. Evaluate your answers (what worked, what didn't)
3. Add any new questions to the bank
4. Update your STAR stories based on what landed

---

**Using Filters**
- Click the filter icon on any column header
- Select specific values to narrow down
- Combine filters (e.g., "Behavioral" + "Final Round")

**Adding Your Own Questions**
- Add new rows at the bottom of each section
- Keep formatting consistent
- Share new questions in the community!

## Tab 5: Quick Reference

### Content Sections

**STAR Method Reminder**
- Situation: Set the scene (brief - 10%)
- Task: Your specific responsibility (brief - 10%)
- Action: What YOU did (detailed - 60%)
- Result: Quantified outcome (strong - 20%)

**Numbers to Have Ready**
- Quota and attainment %
- Average deal size
- Sales cycle length
- Win rate
- YoY growth
- # accounts/opportunities

**Questions That Impress**
1. What does success look like in 90 days?
2. What percentage of the team hit quota?
3. Why is this role open?
4. What do top performers have in common?
5. What would make you say "great hire"?

**Questions to Avoid**
- Basic company info (shows no research)
- Compensation (too early)
- Benefits/perks (too early)
- Time off (too early)
- "What do you do?" (shows no research)

**Answer Structure for Common Questions**

"Tell me about yourself": Present → Past → Future (90 sec)

"Why us?": Research + Fit + Value Add

"Biggest weakness": Real + Managing + Progress

"Why are you looking?": Toward not Away + Specific
